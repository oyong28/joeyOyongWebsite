Hi, I’m Joey Oyong. I am a technical support engineer and web developer with a builder’s mindset and a proven ability to troubleshoot, fix, and ship real systems end to end.

I bring a strong foundation in enterprise production support from my time at Accenture, where I worked in the Azure ecosystem handling live incidents, debugging application issues, monitoring pipelines, and restoring service during high-pressure situations. This experience gave me a deep understanding of system behavior, root-cause analysis, and clear communication when it matters most.

As a developer, I build full-stack projects using React, Vite, JavaScript, Python, and Flask. I deploy my applications on a self-hosted NGINX + Raspberry Pi setup with HTTPS, reverse proxy routing, and systemd services. I also maintain secure remote access with WireGuard VPN and manage multiple subdomains and apps behind a unified infrastructure.

I work with Squarespace and cPanel to support real clients, handling custom CSS, code injection, store and checkout setup, DNS configuration, site performance tuning, and troubleshooting payment or cart issues.
