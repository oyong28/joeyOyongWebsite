Hi, I’m Joey Oyong, a web developer with a builder’s mindset and a proven ability to turn complexity into clarity.

With over a decade of experience in the skilled trades and recent roles in tech, I bring a rare blend of hands-on problem solving, technical discipline, and real-world systems thinking. I transitioned into web development after supporting enterprise cloud infrastructure at Accenture, where I worked on high-stakes incident response and production support in the Azure ecosystem.

Since then, I’ve been focused on developing full-stack projects that reflect both practical needs and modern best practices. I design and deploy fast, responsive, and secure web applications, often self-hosted on my own Raspberry Pi server using tools like Flask, NGINX, WireGuard, and Git. I’ve built automated deployment workflows, configured secure remote access, and implemented backend services to power real user interactions.

What sets me apart is my ability to go beyond tutorials to research, adapt, and build things that work in the wild. I don't just write code. I ship reliable systems, solve problems under pressure, and bring a craftsman’s mentality to everything I do.
